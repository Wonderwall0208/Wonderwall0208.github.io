Chirality Condensed matter Spin transport Phonons

I'm a physics PhD student interested in chiral systems, where mirror and inversion symmetries are both broken. I study how structural chirality shapes the behavior of electrons and phonons.

For more on what I work on, see Research. My papers and talks are listed under Publications, and my study notes are collected in Notes.
